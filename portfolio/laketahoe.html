Lake Tahoe Map

About:

The Lake Tahoe map was created for use by my fellow coworkers for presentation purposes. There is often a need to show Lake Tahoe's location when presenting to audiences outside of the Tahoe region. I created this map using ArcGIS Pro and inspiration from John Nelson's beloved blog.

Sources:

  • OverviewGlobe ArcGIS Pro Project: Blog by John Nelson and ArcGIS Pro Project at https://nation.maps.arcgis.com
  • States: U.S. Census Bureau's TIGER/Line Files at https://hub.arcgis.com/datasets/usdot::states
  • Hillshade: The National Map at https://catalog.data.gov
