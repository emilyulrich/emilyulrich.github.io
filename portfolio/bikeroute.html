Bike Route Map

About:

The Bike Route Map imagines a Tour de France style map on the California coast. It spans from southern California to Vancouver, Canada, covering all kinds of terrain from mountains to valleys. I created this map using ArcGIS Pro, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.

Sources:

  • DEM: Viewfinder Panoramas at http://www.viewfinderpanoramas.org
  • Land Cover Data: UN Food and Agriculture Organization
  • Land and Water Contours: Natural Earth at https://www.naturalearthdata.com
  • Bike Route Creation: Google My Maps at https://www.google.com/maps/d/
  • States Data: NDOT GeoHub at https://hub.arcgis.com/datasets/NDOT::usa-states-generalized-option-1
