Planet Exo Map

About:

The Planet Exo map was created through Mapbox to represent an imagined version of a Star Trek planet. The map illustrates an icy, blue planet with mysterious purple waters. My inspiration for the location of this map is based off of my undergraduate trip to Siberia. Yakutsk is the coldest city in Siberia, which made it the perfect starting location for a cold, icy planet.
